Second day of Dussehra

  • Form:

    Represents penance and knowledge.

  • Symbolism:

    Self-discipline and commitment.

  • Rituals:

    Fasting, meditation, and recitation of scriptures.

  • Image of Second Day Goddess in Dussehra

  • The Second day of Dussehra is Typically depicted wearing a white sari, she carries a rosary in one hand and a water pot in the other.
