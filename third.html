Third day of Dussehra

  • Form:

    Fierce and valiant goddess.

  • Symbolism:

    Courage and bravery

  • Rituals:

    Special prayers for strength and protection

  • Image of Third Day Goddess in Dussehra

  • The Third day of Dussehra is Depicted with a crescent moon on her forehead, she rides a tiger or lion and holds various weapons.
