First day of Dussehra

  • Form:

    Goddess of the mountains, represents strength

  • Symbolism:

    Devotion and purity.

  • Rituals:

    Offerings of flowers, fruits, and chanting of hymns.

  • Image of First Day Goddess in Dussehra

  • The first day of Dussehra is called Kunwar Amavasya, and it is the night when the state's control is formally transferred to the Diwan. The ceremony includes asking for permission from a girl who is believed to have spiritual powers, and who is seen in a war-like posture with a wooden sword.
